# Product Guidelines

## 1. Tone of Voice

Our brand voice is **friendly and approachable**. We want to connect with our users on a personal level and make them feel comfortable using our product. We avoid overly formal language and corporate jargon.

## 2. Writing Style

Our writing style is **detailed and technical**. We believe in providing our users with all the information they need to succeed. We use clear and concise language, but we are not afraid to go into detail when necessary. We use technical terms where appropriate, but we always explain them clearly.

## 3. Formatting

We use a **specific heading structure** to make our content easy to read and scan. All content should follow a logical hierarchy, with clear and descriptive headings.

- **H1:** Used for main titles.
- **H2:** Used for major sections.
- **H3:** Used for sub-sections.

## 4. Brand Messaging

Our brand messaging is centered around the idea of **empowering businesses through technology**. We want to help our users save time, increase efficiency, and grow their businesses. Our messaging should always be positive, optimistic, and focused on the benefits of our product.
